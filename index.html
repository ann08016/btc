What We Do?

Senior Care Services

Wellness

Wellness services include Thyroid and Metabolism, Weight Management, Digestive Health, Immune Health, Joints and Repair, Cardiovascular Fitness and Comprehensive Diabetic Management and lifestyle support to help residents manage glucose levels and optimize health and well-being. The staff can design a program fitness that's just for you.

Companionship

Companionship services include meal preparation, light housekeeping, errands, shopping and of course companionship. If your family member, friend or loved one enjoys their independence but struggling with everyday activities, companionship services is the solution. Your Home Health Aide (HHA), Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN), Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) or caregiver will manage their daily requirements with specialized companion services, matching their needs with the best available caregiver. If you choose to allow Born to Care to provide elderly companionship services for loved one, you'll know you've provided them with the best possible care available.

Physical Assistance

Physical Assistance covers a variety of scenarios. Most common are: Medication Reminders. Incontinence Care, Bathing, Grooming, Dressing, Transfers to Toilet, Bed, Car, Fall Prevention and even Prescribed Exercise Programs. Born To Care professionals understand the sensitivity of personal care and will be compassionate and respectful. Starting Physical Assistance services will make sure your loved one is living safely and comfortably in their own home.

Hygiene

The most important aspect of maintaining good health is good personal hygiene. Personal hygiene which is also referred to as personal care includes the following: Bathing and Showering, Hair Care, Nail Care, Foot Care, Genital Care and Dental Care. Personal hygiene is keeping the body clean, and helps prevent the spread of germs. Grooming is caring for fingernails and hair examples of these activities would be styling hair, shaving, trimming and painting fingernails. Maintaining good health also includes the following areas: Nutrition, Leisure/ Recreation Opportunities, Sleep, and Exercise. As you can see, there are many factors that contribute to feeling and looking good. Feeling and looking good are important to each individual's emotional and physical well-being.
